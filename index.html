Otherworldly

I remember when
I was 8 and the world spun high around me
and I could never catch it on its axis

I remember when
I was 11 and I was suddenly presented with
options; trajectories I could waltz with
and I couldn't pick just one!

I remember when
I could walk straight at 14
and followed the equator because
it was right under my feet

The world made sense to me
and I tilted with it just right
